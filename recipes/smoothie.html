Strawberry banana smoothie

Description

Try out this delicious strawberry smoothie. Made with strawberries, bananas, sweet vanilla yogurt and honey. You can make this smoothie in no time!

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups vanilla yogurt
  • 2 bananas, cut up
  • ½ cup frozen strawberries
  • 2 tablespoons wheat germ
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Steps

  1. Combine the yogurt, bananas, strawberries, wheat germ, and honey in a blender
  2. Blend until smooth, about 1 minute

Enjoy!
